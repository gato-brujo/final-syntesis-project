Big Bang Evidence

The big bang theory is the one that astronomers use to explain the origin of the universe, it explains that everything began in a single point that suddently exploded and gave shape to the things we know that are in the universe nowadays. However, there are a lot of people that deny this theory, therefore in this article there is going to be some evidence of the big bang for those who are not completely convinced.

Redshift of galaxies

In 1929, the astronomer Edwin Hubble declared that almost all galaxies seem to be moving away from us, in point of fact that was the first indicator that the universe was moving and expanding. Hubble used spectroscopic red-shift data to measure the speed in which objects in the universe were moving and the far they were from earth. He realized that the farther a galaxy is from us, the faster it is moving away from earth. So knowing that this red-shift is from galaxies getting away from us if we went backwards in time we would see galaxies getting closer to us (we are getting closer to them as well), and if we were back enough all in the universe would be in the same place.
